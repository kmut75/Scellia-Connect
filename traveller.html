How it works

Simple. Secure. 4 steps.

From sign-up to payment — everything is handled by Scellia.

1
📋

You post your flight

Enter your route, departure date and available kilos. The platform handles matching automatically.

2
🔐

You accept a request

Review the parcel description, photos and weight. You accept or decline freely — no obligation.

3
✈️

You carry

The money is already in escrow. You hand the parcel to the recipient and confirm delivery with an OTP.

4
💰

You get paid

The escrow is released automatically after confirmation. Transfer to your account within 24–48h.

Safety

You are protected at every step.

Scellia is designed so you take no customs, financial or personal risk.

📷

Mandatory photos of contents

The sender must provide photos of the open parcel before sending. You see exactly what you are carrying.

🚫

Right to refuse total

You can refuse any request, at any time, without explanation. Your decision is final.

🔒

Escrow avant le vol

Payment is locked in from acceptance. You never depend on the sender's goodwill to get paid.

📜

Prohibited items list

A clear list of non-accepted items (cash, official documents, regulated products) is included with every mission.

🎉

Verified profiles

Every sender is verified (ID, phone number, address) before they can post a request.

🛡

€500 insurance included

In case of loss or damage on arrival, insurance covers up to €500 per mission.
